Orange Juice


Squeezing fruit to drink the liquid inside is something that humans and animals have done for thousands of years, but
probably no other type of juice is as popular as orange juice. Although the United States remains the largest consumer of orange juice, it is prevalent around the world, as it not only helps quench thirst but also provides an instant boost in energy and increases the metabolic rate.
Orange juice can boast various health benefits and is an excellent source of vitamin C, which is why so many people
choose to drink it as part of their breakfast.

Mixed nuts


Nuts are an ideal nutritious snack.They’re linked to a reduced
risk of heart disease and may help prevent certain cancers,
depression, and other illnesses.
Despite being relatively high in fat, they are very filling.
Several studies suggest that eating nuts in moderation can
help you lose weight.
Nuts provide the perfect balance of healthy fat, protein, and
fiber. They contain 180 calories in a 1-ounce (28-gram)
serving, on average.
Because they don’t require refrigeration, they’re perfect for
taking on the go.

Alexis Bistro’s sinful Tiramisu cake


Alexis has been around for awhile. But in case you need an introduction, they’re renowned among KL-ites for regular live jazz performances. Much like the sophisticated vibes you get
from jazz music, their Tiramisu (RM17.80) is all glammed up
with a crusty top layer of caramelised almonds and pecans.
Layered between the coffee and Cognac-soaked cake is a generous spread of mascarpone cheese
that’s embedded with dark chocolate shavings. It comes with a side of tangy strawberry compote that complements its
sweetness, so that you’ll always have room for another slice.
